A Word From Editor

Welcome to the October issue of P2 Magazine.

This issue we cover a whole range of topics that ThoughtWorkers are talking about from the reality of IoT by Mat Henshall to gaming your security training with Rosalie Tolentino. There are often a few contraversial topics discussed when we decide what goes on the ThoughtWorks Tech Radar. Kevin Dishman and Wendy Isanavic would like to share some of that with you in BDDon't and Lombok: A Love/Hate Relationship.

Finally, Abby Bangser explains how to treat DevOps stories just like real stories and Luiza Nunes shows you how to take down your QA lane!

As always if you would like to contribute please reach out to us on p2@thoughtworks.com.

Thank you for reading and for the great feedback you’ve given the P2 team.

Rachel Laycock, Editor in Chief
